This is a historical dictionary of hip-hop

As of today, there are {{ entry_count }} entries. Each of these has a headword, part of speech, & one or more senses.

A sense is a meaning of a word. Dope, for example, has at least two senses:

1. drugs, as in:

All I love's my dope and dead presidents

&

2. good, exciting, etc., as in:

Bear witness to the dopest fuckin rhyme I wrote

Every sense has a definition. I don't always get these right, so please let me know if you see something wrong.
